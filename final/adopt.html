Adoption

Every year, there are over 6.5 million animals enter shelters across the US. Of the 6.5 million animals, approximately 3.2 million are cats, and 860,000 of them are euthanized. By choosing to adopt a shelter or rescue cat, you are saving more than one life! When you adopt, you are not only giving an animal a loving home, but you are also opening up shelter space for another animal in need.

(Source)

If you are planning on getting a pet, consider adopt one and save lives!
